"A Grandmother's Journey for Survival"

Every morning, a grandmother and her young grandchild walk the streets of Barangay Balintawak, bags slung over their shoulders as they collect anything of value to sell. For them, each day is a battle for survival relying on scraps to earn just enough to feed themselves. Their story is one of perseverance, reflecting the countless families who struggle daily to overcome the weight of poverty.

"Dreams on Hold"

In the heart of Barangay Balintawak, a group of children gather in an alley, their laughter masking the harsh reality they face. Unlike other children, they don’t have the luxury of attending school. Financial hardship has stolen their right to an education, leaving them with dreams that feel increasingly out of reach. Still, they find joy in each other's company, unaware that the world outside their neighborhood offers a future they might never experience.

"A Mother's Unyielding Hope"

Amid the clutter of recyclable materials, a mother sits exhausted, her hands calloused from hours of scavenging. Every piece she collects is another step closer to securing her children’s future. Despite the overwhelming challenges, her resolve never falters. She dreams of seeing her children graduate and break free from the cycle of poverty that has defined her own life. Her sacrifice is a powerful reminder of the lengths parents will go to give their children a better chance in life.
